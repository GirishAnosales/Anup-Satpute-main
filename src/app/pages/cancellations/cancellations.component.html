Cancelling / replacing product(s) in your order

Any product in the tentative Bill of Quantity can be replaced or removed while you're still discussing designs with your design team i.e. till the project order (50%) is not created. ANUPLIFESTYLE cannot offer to cancel, replace or modify items once the final order is placed i.e. a sales order is created Because each item is made to order. However, please be assured that your design team will always explicitly discuss all items with you before placing orders.

Why is only half of your booking amount is refunded?

ANUPLIFESTYLE takes pride in its exemplary design service. As each client gets access to a personal design and project team who carefully work on designs and concepts for their home, putting in a lot of time and hard work, we are unable to offer a full refund after booking.
