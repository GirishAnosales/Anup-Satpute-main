Payment terms

You can book your decor project by paying a booking fee which is 10% of the estimated order value. The booking fee is adjusted against future orders of the project.

Here, A project refers to home design undertaking for a single home. Clint can place Multiple orders for a single project (for eg., one order for modular products, a separate one for services such as civil work). Payments are processed on an order level.

Execution milestones for an order

Mentioned below are the types of orders and pre-defined execution stages for a clear picture of your payment schedule. At the execution stages, 40 - 50% of the work in terms of the order value, is completed. Once the order reaches this stage, the balance 50% payment is due. Payment terms for an order In case your booking fee is already adjusted against an order(s), the following payment terms will apply for all subsequent orders. Need to be placed at 50% payment.
